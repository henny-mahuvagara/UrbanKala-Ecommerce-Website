Our Story

Urban Kala was born from a deep respect for India's artistic legacy and a desire to bring it into the modern home.

We bridge the gap between skilled artisans and modern consumers who value authenticity. Every product in our collection is a testament to culture, passion, and timeless artistry.

By choosing Urban Kala, you're not just buying a product; you're supporting livelihoods, preserving traditional art forms, and celebrating India’s rich cultural heritage.
